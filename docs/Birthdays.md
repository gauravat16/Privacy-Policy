# Privacy Policy for Birthdays!

This page describes the permissions used by the Birthdays! app and why they are needed.

## Permissions

### Read Contacts

This permission is used to get contact names and phone numbers when you add a new birthday from your contacts.

### SMS

This permission is used to send birthday wishes by SMS when you choose to use that feature.

### Storage

This permission is used to save and restore backup files for your birthday list.
